The Protocol

How ForkIt Works

A fully on-chain order lifecycle. From escrow lock to atomic settlement — every step verified, every payment trustless.

01
Customer Places Order
Funds + 2% security deposit locked in a Solana escrow PDA. USDC or EURC — your choice. 15-minute funding window with auto-refund.
02
Restaurant Accepts & Prepares
Restaurant sees the funded order, accepts, and marks food ready for pickup. Driver gets notified automatically.
03
Driver Picks Up (Code A)
Driver verifies pickup by submitting Code A hash on-chain. Human or AI-routed delivery — both earn loyalty points.
04
Customer Confirms (Code B)
Customer enters Code B on delivery. Atomic settlement: restaurant paid, driver paid, 0.02% fee to treasury, deposit refunded.
// Example: Pizza Night with friends 🍕

  items: ["Margherita x2"],

Three Roles

One App. Every Role.

ForkMe — the mobile companion — serves customers, drivers, and restaurants from a single codebase on iOS, Android, and Solana Seeker.

🛒
Customer

Order food, split costs with friends, track your delivery live. Your funds never leave escrow until the food arrives.

  • Browse restaurants & menus
  • Place orders with USDC or EURC
  • Share contribution links to split payment
  • Live map tracking via Socket.IO
  • Confirm delivery with Code B
  • Earn loyalty points per order
🚗
Driver

Find available funded orders, claim deliveries, and get paid atomically on-chain. No platform taking your cut.

  • Browse available funded orders
  • Claim deliveries in your zone
  • Verify pickup with Code A (QR or text)
  • AI-routed orders earn +50% bonus points
  • On-chain reputation badge
  • Instant settlement after delivery
🍽️
Restaurant

Manage incoming orders, set your own delivery zones and pricing, accept the tokens you want. No monthly fees.

  • Dashboard for incoming funded orders
  • IPFS-hosted menu with images
  • Accept USDC, EURC, or both
  • Whitelist mints via on-chain config
  • Mark ready for pickup
  • Rate drivers post-delivery
Split Payments

Anyone Can Chip In

Share a deep link, friends contribute directly to the escrow. Up to 10 contributors per order. Deposits returned proportionally. Trustless by design — the app never touches the money.



The escrow holds all funds. Anyone can call contribute_to_order directly — no trust required. Cancelled or timed-out orders return every contributor's share automatically.
